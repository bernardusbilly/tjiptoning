History
1958
Founded by the late Mr. Setyadi Tjiptoning. It was then named Biro Bangunan Tjiptoning.
1975
Changed its name to CV Tjiptoning.
1979
Changed its name to PT. Tjiptoning Sejati to present day.
1989
Board of Director position passed on to Mr. Rudy Tjiptoning and Mr. Eddy Tjiptoning.
1998
Associated with Asano Engineering Co. - JAPAN.
1999
Associated with Ando Corporation - JAPAN (JICA project).
2014
Expanded into Real Estate investments.
